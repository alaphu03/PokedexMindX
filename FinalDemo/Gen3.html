Hoenn Pokedex

The Hoenn Pokédex was introduced in Generation III and contains 202 Pokémon in Generation III and 211 Pokémon in Generation VI, starting with Treecko, who is one of the Starter Pokémon of that generation and ending with Deoxys who is a Mythical Pokémon. The Hoenn Pokédex is featured a total of two generation of games, Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire, and Pokémon Emerald and Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire. It first appeared in one Nintendo platform, the Game Boy Advance. It can also be accessed in the Game Boy slot of the Original and Nintendo DS Lite, and Game Boy Player. Alternatively, the GameCube game Pokémon Box Ruby/Sapphire comes with an emulator for Ruby and Sapphire and has its own 3D models of the Generation three Pokémon, which can be considered a separate Pokédex. The later releases Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire however are used only on the Nintendo 3DS or 2DS.
