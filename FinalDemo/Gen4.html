Sinnoh Pokedex

The Sinnoh Pokédex was introduced in Generation IV which contains 151 Pokémon in Pokémon Diamond and Pearl and 210 Pokémon in Pokémon Platinum, starting with Turtwig, one of the Starter Pokémon, and ending with Manaphy (in Diamond and Pearl), a Mythical Pokémon and Giratina (in Platinum), a Legendary Pokémon. Additionally, seven more Sinnoh based Pokémon is not featured in the Sinnoh Pokédex. These are Arceus, Shaymin, Heatran, Phione, Darkrai, Cresselia and Regigigas.
