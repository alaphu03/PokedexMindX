Unova Pokedex

The Unova Pokédex was introduced in Generation V which contains 156 Pokémon starting with Victini, who is #000, and ends with Genesect, who is #155, both are Mythical Pokémon. It is the only Pokédex to contain Pokémon only found in that Generation (with the exception of Generation I). This Pokédex also has the most Pokémon of all Generations (with Generation V overtaking Generation I for most Pokémon in a Generation). Also, in the games Pokémon Black 2 and White 2, the sequels of the originals Gen V games, the Unova Pokédex features 300 Pokémon from different Regions before beating the game, making the Unova Pokédex the biggest Pokédex before the National Pokédex.

The Unova Pokédex is featured in the games: Pokémon Black and White & Pokémon Black 2 and Pokémon White 2 (with a different sets of Pokémon).
